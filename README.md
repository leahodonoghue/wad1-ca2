- This is an app where users can keep a collection of their favourite cities they have been to

- the collections are categorized by country
- For each city you enter the name and what type of city it is (Coastal City, Capital City etc...) what its most famous for and give a rating and add an image
- users can serach through their collections and also add new countries or delete existing countries
